Sip, Savor, and Socialize at Broomfield!

Located near the Broomfield park in Palmers Green, Broomfield Cafe and Bar is your go-to destination for delightful brews, scrumptious bites, and memorable moments. Whether you're catching up with friends, enjoying a solo coffee break, or winding down after work, Broomfield offers a cozy ambiance and a diverse menu to cater to all your cravings.

history

A Legacy by the Park

Broomfield Cafe and Bar carries with it a rich history intertwined with the tales of Broomfield Park. Over the years, we've witnessed countless stories, from park strolls turning into lifelong partnerships to families marking milestones. Our cafe is a testament to the timeless charm of Palmers Green.

comunity

Broomfield's Heartbeat

At Broomfield Cafe and Bar, we believe in fostering a sense of community. Located just a stone's throw away from Broomfield Park, we've become a gathering spot for locals and visitors alike. Our cafe isn't just a place to dine; it's where friendships bloom and memories are made.

philosophy

Authenticity in Every Sip

Our philosophy is simple: genuine service, authentic flavors, and a commitment to our community. Every cup of coffee, every meal, and every interaction at Broomfield Cafe and Bar is steeped in authenticity. We're not just serving food and drinks; we're sharing a piece of our heart with you.
